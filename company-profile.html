Company Profile

PAL Trade Chem is a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) registered under the 2008 LLP Act and is a Government of India (MCA) approved trading house for industrial and chemical goods.

ABOUT PAL Trade Chem LLP

PAL Trade Chem LLP is a Certified Importer for Industrial Goods and Chemical Trading. PAL Trade Chem LLP head office is situated in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. We are operational in PAN India for Industrial Goods and Chemical needs.

We also accommodate certain clients' specialised needs and give them access to solutions that meet their professional criteria. Producing and trading chemicals of the highest quality, which are profitable commodities, is the forte of professionals at PAL Trade Chem LLP. We are quickly acquiring popularity in the relevant markets due to our high effectiveness in promptly satisfying all bulk customer requests.

As a result, we can easily meet all the urgent needs of customers without any delays or compromises because we always have a big amount of quality-assured & well-packaged products on hand. In addition, a lot of businesses and people are joining forces with us because of our profitable business transactions and ethical and transparent company procedures.

Pal Trade Chem LLP is a Certified Importer and an Authorized Sub Distributor for Domestic or Public Sector Units under a Registered Dealership.
